Our Company

Prolux Lighting Concepts is a leading provider of decorative home lighting solutions in India. Established over 20 years ago, we have grown to become one of the most trusted names in the industry. With multiple showrooms and distributors across the country, we offer a wide range of lighting products for residential and commercial spaces. Our extensive product range includes chandeliers, wall lights, floor lamps, table lamps, and more. We also offer customized lighting solutions to meet the unique needs of our clients, including interior designers, architects, and home users. Our commitment to quality, affordability, and customer satisfaction has made us a preferred choice for discerning customers across India.
